Nice to Meet You...

Hi, I'm Reham Shadi. I founded Interior Monkey in 2011 to make it possible for startups and small businesses to create their dream office in a way they can afford. I believe that amazing spaces can be created on modest budgets and big ideas.

I live in Oakland with my husband Ed and our chihuahua, Gus. Prior to moving to the Bay Area in 2010, I lived and worked as a commercial and residential interior designer for 10 years in Los Angeles, New York, Columbus, and Cairo. I love to travel, I love to make things with my hands, and I know my way around a flea market. I have a bit of an obsession with mid century chairs...you'll notice this when I get carried away and go on and on as I tell you about the transition from a 4-star to a 5-star base...

Reham Shadi, LEED AP
