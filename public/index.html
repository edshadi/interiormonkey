Nice to Meet You...

Hi, I'm Reham Shadi. I founded Interior Monkey in 2011 to make it possible for startups and small businesses to create their dream office in a way they can afford. I believe that amazing spaces can be created on modest budgets and big ideas.

I'm based between the San Francisco Bay Area and Barcelona with my husband Ed and our chihuahua, Gus. I lived and worked as a commercial and residential interior designer for over 10 years in the San Francisco Bay Area, Barcelona, Los Angeles, New York, Columbus, and Cairo. I love to travel, I love to make things with my hands, and I know my way around a flea market. I have a bit of an obsession with mid century chairs...you'll notice this when I get carried away and go on and on as I tell you about the transition from a 4-star to a 5-star base...
